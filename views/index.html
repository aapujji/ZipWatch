What's ZipWatch?

The Zipwatch project provides a decision-making tool for those who are in the market to buy or rent a home. We evaluate each neighborhood in the NYC area based on statistical factors. Using this information, we determine the quality of life in each neighborhood, displaying the facts in comprehensive visuals.

This app was created by students in the Hunter College Computer Science department.
